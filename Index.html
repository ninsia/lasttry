What does color mean?

Color is your brain's interpretation of the signals your eyes give off when light enters. That's why you don't see colors when it's dark. In fact, we can only detect red, green, and blue light with the rods and cones in our eyes.

FLOWER COLORS

With flowers it is possible to convey a message. The color of flowers also has a certain meaning.

People have used the symbolism of flowers for centuries. A certain type or color is carefully selected to give a bouquet of flowers more power.

Red flowers are widely used when giving romantic gifts to a loved one. They represent love, romance, and passion. Red flowers are often used to convey luxury and to show that you care about someone.

Orange flowers are often used for congratulations and birthdays. They stand for cheerfulness, warmth, coziness, pleasure, strength, vitality and energy. Orange flowers are most common in the autumn months.

Yellow flowers represent energy, strength, growth, cheerfulness and joy. Yellow also represents warmth, sun and new beginnings. They also go well with the start of spring. For example, suitable for: birthday and congratulations.

Green flowers are often used to congratulate someone on her pregnancy, but also to wish someone good luck and strength. The color green represents growth, hope and new life. On the other hand, green flowers symbolize fruitfulness, young life and courage.

Blue flowers symbolize divinity, innocence, space and eternity. For example, blue flowers can be given at the birth of a child. It symbolizes trust, loyalty, wisdom, self-assurance, intelligence, faith, truth and heaven.

Purple flowers are often used in mourning and condolence. Purple flowers are also given to wish someone good luck and at New Year. Purple flowers represent dignity, seriousness and mourning. The blue flowers are often used at the birth of a boy.

Pink flowers, like white, represent innocence. This color also represents romance and tenderness. With this color you really say: "I love you". For example, a pink bouquet is suitable for the birth of a girl.

White flowers are often used at weddings, if you want to wish someone strength or at a birth. They represent purity, freshness, clarity and simplicity. They can also have other meanings such as: calmness, innocence, virginity, natural love and innocence.

CHAKRA COLORS

A chakra can be seen as a wheel of energy within the body. Each chakra represents a specific part of the body with an associated color.

The Seven Chakras colors are Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indigo, Violet. The order of the chakra is from the lower root chakra to the upper crown chakra.

I. The color red belongs to the root chakra. Red gives a feeling of warmth, strength, love and energy. On the other hand, red also represents anger, destruction and danger. The first chakra is the root chakra also called Muladhara in Sanskrit. The root chakra is located in the perineum at the bottom of the spine. This chakra represents orientation and provides a safe feeling. By staying positive and open to new opportunities, this chakra maintains its power.

II. The color orange belongs to the sacral chakra. Orange gives a feeling of energy, willpower, creativity and enthusiasm. The second chakra is the sacral chakra also called Swadhisthana in Sanskrit. The sacral chakra is located just below the navel and is connected to the genitals, kidneys, bladder and uterus. This chakra represents balance on both an emotional and sexual level. It gives you strength to enter into relationships with other people in a healthy way.

III. The color yellow belongs to the solar plexus chakra. Yellow represents cheerfulness, wisdom and optimism. In addition to positive effects, yellow also stands for disagreement, dominance and aggression. The third chakra is the solar plexus chakra, also called Manipura in Sanskrit. This chakra is located at the level of the navel and is linked to the stomach, small intestine, liver and spleen. This chakra represents self-confidence and purpose. With a well-developed solar plexus chakra, the I-feeling is strongly present. You can be there for others, but also for yourself.

IV. The color green belongs to the heart chakra. Green represents calmness, hope and peace. Green also stands for jealousy, envy and superstition. The fourth chakra is the heart chakra also called Anahata in Sanskrit. The heart chakra is located in the center of the chest, just next to the heart. The Anahata is linked to the heart, lungs and breathing. This chakra is the center of the body. The heart chakra is the connecting piece between the top 3 and the bottom 3 chakras. It stands for intimacy, security and love. Not only for yourself but also love for others.

V. The color blue belongs to the throat chakra. Blue stands for tranquility, independence, creativity and sincerity. On the other hand, blue also represents depression and fatigue. The fifth chakra is the throat chakra also called Vishuddhi in Sanskrit. The throat chakra is (unsurprisingly) located at the throat and is linked to the vocal cords, mouth, cervical vertebrae and the thyroid gland. This chakra represents communication and expression of emotions. It's about what we say and how we say it. The feeling that arises when making sounds. Not only is your own communication important, but also that of the other person. Learn to calm down and listen.

VI. The color indigo belongs to the third eye. Indigo stands for perception, cooling and forms the interface between emotional and spiritual events. The sixth chakra is the third eye also called Ajna chakra in Sanskrit. The third eye is located in the center of the forehead and is connected to the pineal gland, eyes, face and nervous system. This chakra represents imagination, wisdom and insight. The third eye catches events that we cannot perceive with our eyes. Actually, this chakra sees through feelings.

VII. The color violet belongs to the crown chakra. Violet represents spirituality, self-observation and conviction. On the other hand, violet also represents arrogance.The seventh chakra is the crown chakra also called Sahasrara in Sanskrit. The crown chakra is located on top of the head, or crown. It is connected to the cerebral cortex and nervous system. This chakra is often associated with spirituality and connects to the universe. You can see it as a gateway to what is happening outside of us.

Green eyed people are lucky people. They are seen as sexy, seductive and mysterious. They are intelligent and always curious, and they like to try something new.

Brown eyed people are considered loyal, trustworthy, respectful, and friendly - but by no means submissive.
